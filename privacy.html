privacy

The short version: we never see anything you write.

Enough does not collect, transmit, or store any user data on external servers. All entries remain on your device. We have never seen, and cannot see, anything you write.

a little more, if you want it

Enough has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind. There is no backend that could be hacked or subpoenaed, because there is no backend.

Your entries, your photos, and your settings live on your iPhone. They are backed up by iCloud only if you have iCloud Backup enabled in your iOS settings — and that backup is encrypted by Apple, not by us. We have no access to it.

Enough does not use any third-party SDKs. No Crashlytics, no Firebase, no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no nothing. The only thing the app does over the network is allow you to tap "contact" and open your default mail client to write us a note. That communication, if it happens, goes through your mail provider, not through us.

If you ever uninstall Enough, your data is deleted with it. If you have iCloud Backup, restoring your phone will restore your entries. If you don't, they're gone, and that's between you and your decision to delete.

We don't collect anything because we don't want to. There is no future version of Enough that adds analytics or tracking — that would betray the entire point of the product.
