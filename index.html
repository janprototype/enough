why

If life is going well, that itself is a great accomplishment.

Most apps are built on the quiet assumption that you are lacking something — that you should be doing more, tracking more, optimizing more. This one isn't.

Once a day, Enough asks you a small question. You answer in a sentence. Then it closes. There is nothing else to do.

After a few months, you can scroll back and see what your life has actually been — not the highlights, not the wins, just the ordinary good. Most people don't realize when they're already fine.

What does it actually do?
Asks you one question a day. You answer in a sentence. After a few months, you have a quiet record of your ordinary life.
Where does my data go?
Nowhere. Entries stay on your phone. We've never seen a single one. There are no servers to leak, no accounts to hack.
How often will it bother me?
Once a day, at a time you choose. The notification is one word. That's the only one Enough will ever send.
Why does it cost anything?
Because nothing is free. We'd rather you pay us once than have you become the product. No subscription, no upgrades, no ads — ever.
What if I miss a day?
Nothing happens. There are no streaks to break. Come back when you come back.
